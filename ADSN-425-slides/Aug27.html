MP1 - 2 Second Loop

Mess around with frame-animation techniques in Photoshop and create a two second (48 frame) looping animation. Think about using timing as a design tool – for example what does negative space look like when applied to time?

The point of this assignment is to get used to the tools, so do not put too much pressure on the outcome.
