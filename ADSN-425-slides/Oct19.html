Mini Project 7 – Animated Letterforms

This project is meant to get you thinking about ways to use 3D software to animate images.

You will be assigned 2 capital letterforms – see Discord to know which ones. Your task is to create a compelling 2–3-second long animation for each letterform. You can use 3D modeling (meshes, curves, metaballs), materials/textures, layering – or any other tools and techniques to create and animate your letterforms. You can use blender's in-built text. But make it your own.

Think of a mood (like spooky for October maybe). Design one of your letters to evoke that mood. Design the other letter to evoke an opposite mood (like comforting in the case of spooky).

I will compile these into the whole alphabet before the end of the semester.

 

Format

  • For each letter: 1200 × 1200 pixel .mp4 video file
  • Upload videos to Canvas and Discord (if they are not too big)
